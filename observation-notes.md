# Observation Notes

- Daily AQI data was collected for 10 sample days.
- Observed that 3 out of 10 days exceeded safe levels (>100 AQI).
- Noted variability in daily air quality and importance of tracking trends.
- Highlighted the need for real-time monitoring to support public health decisions.

## Limitations
The dataset is limited in size and used only for demonstrating analysis methods,
not for drawing real-world conclusions.
